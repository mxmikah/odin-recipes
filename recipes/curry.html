Japanese Beef Curry

Description

With tender beef, potatoes, carrots, mushrooms, and curry roux, this savory and hearty Japanese Beef Curry makes for a fabulous introduction to new curry rice eaters. Adapted to Japanese tastes , its mild and sweet with a stew-like consistency. Even children enjoy it thoroughly! You have to give this easy recipe a try.

Ingredients

  • Beef (typically chuck roast)
  • Onion, potatoes, and carrot
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Wine
  • Beef stock or broth
  • Seasionings: tomato paste, japanese curry powder, Japanese curry roux, and bay leaf

Directions

  1. Cut the onions and start sauteing them until tender and caramalized.
  2. Meanwhile, cut all the vegetabels and beef
  3. Sear the beef and add it to the caramelized onions.
  4. Add the wine, stock, carrots, mushrooms and seasionings, and cook for 2 hours. (Add the potatoes 15 minutes prior to finishing cooking in order to save their shape).
  5. Add Japanese curry roux and server with rice!
